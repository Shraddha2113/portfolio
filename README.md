## ☕️ Project 1: The "Mid-Week Study Oasis" Campaign
* **Client:** The Daily Grind Coffee Co. (Mock Project)
* **My Role:** Social Media Strategist & Content Creator
* **The Goal:** Increase weekday afternoon foot traffic from local college students using a targeted 1-week Instagram campaign.

### 🎯 The Strategy
The Daily Grind is located right near a college campus but struggles with empty tables on Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons. To fix this, I designed a 3-part Instagram campaign positioning the shop as the ultimate student study escape with targeted incentives.

---

### 📱 The Content Breakdown

#### 📮 Post 1: The Aesthetic Invitation
* **Visual Concept:** A cozy photo of a corner table with a laptop, notebooks, and a fresh iced latte.
* **Main Headline:** "Binge after class? ☕️"
* **Caption:** "Swap the noisy campus library for your new favorite study escape. We’ve got fast Wi-Fi, endless outlets, and the caffeine boost you need to survive finals week. Come find your cozy corner at The Daily Grind today. 📚💻"

#### 📮 Post 2: The Mid-Week BOGO Offer
* **Visual Concept:** A quick video clip of a barista pouring milk into a beautiful matcha latte.
* **Main Headline:** "Thirsty noon, hungry studies? 🕒📚"
* **Caption:** "Tuesday afternoon blues? ☕️ Bring your study buddy to The Daily Grind between 2 PM and 6 PM on Tuesdays or Wednesdays, show your student ID, and get Buy One, Get One 50% Off on all large drinks!"

#### 📮 Post 3: The Student Review (Social Proof)
* **Visual Concept:** A clean, colorful graphic showing a 5-star rating layout.
* **Main Headline:** "Too cliché? Hear from them. 💬"
* **Review:** *"The best iced vanilla latte in town, and the seating is so comfortable. My new go-to study spot!"* — Sarah, University Junior
* **Caption:** "Don't take our word for it—ask Sarah! 💻 Drop by this week and see why we're the neighborhood's favorite hidden gem."

---

### 📊 Campaign Results (Estimated)
* **Foot Traffic:** Weekday afternoon traffic among college students increased by **35%**.
* **Offer Redemptions:** **84 student pairs** claimed the BOGO discount code.
* **Account Growth:** Generated a **12% increase** in local Instagram followers over 7 days.
